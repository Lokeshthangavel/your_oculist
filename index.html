Vision Test

Please follow the instructions below to complete the vision test.

Instructions:

  1. Position yourself approximately 6 meters (20 feet) away from your screen.
  2. If you wear glasses or contacts, remove them for this test.
  3. Cover your left eye and read the smallest line you can see clearly with your right eye.
  4. Repeat for your left eye (covering your right eye).
  5. Select the smallest line you could read for each eye in the form below.
